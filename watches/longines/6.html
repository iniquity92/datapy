  • About Longines Watches

    Longines is one of the oldest watchmakers based in Saint-Imier, Switzerland since 1832. Their watch collection reflects a firm commitment to tradition, sophistication and performance. With numerous years of experience as a timekeeper for championships in sports or as an accomplice of global games league, Longines – a much-admired brand for the classiness of its timepieces – is a part of the Swatch Group Ltd, the world's primary manufacturer of horological items. Longines uses its patent signature, “Elegance is an Attitude”, as a parameter for crafting all its products. Its timepieces are motivated by the historical backdrop of nominal and stylistic daring of an organization which persistently screens its creations with a sharp eye. Today, Longines produces timepieces meeting requirements of the present day yet regarding the qualities which it has maintained in the past years. They enclose a remarkable range of watches in the Elegance, Watchmaking Tradition, Sport and Heritage collection. Longines is glad to incorporate the British actress Kate Winslet, Bollywood icon Aishwarya Rai Bachchan amongst its Ambassadors of Elegance. The Asian actress and model Ching Lin Ling, Chinese star Aaron Kwok Fu Shing, and Russian actress Ingeborga Dapkunaite, perfectly picture the standards upheld and promoted by Longines.
