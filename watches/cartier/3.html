  • About Cartier Watches

    A renowned French brand, Cartier watches are just as magnificent as their jewellery. Representing all that dreams are made of, their spectacular timepieces are enchanting and splendid, enough to leave one mesmerized. Through time and over centuries, Cartier has built a rich and eventful heritage, so glorious that it speaks for itself. After realising that the wrist watches are the future, Louis Cartier created a Santos watch in 1904 for the pioneering aviator Albert Santos-Dumont. Its design was modern and had a perfect balance of form and function. Masters at creating stunning jewellery watches, they have changed the way women look at precious ornaments. Cartier was the first brand to explore femininity by comparing it with a Panther. A founding name in haute horlogerie, the brand has carved a niche not only in aesthetic, embellished timepieces but also in innovative and radical ones. Constantly strengthening and improvising their watch making inheritance vis-à-vis knowledge and skills, Cartier continues to awe the horological world with their revolutionary creations. The timepieces so crafted are an amalgam of classical and conventional methods. Tradition, together with exceptionally creative novelty has been the hallmark of Cartier since it was established. Beauty and technical perfection have been their key parameters and they speak of unparalleled precision, grand mechanics and fine quality. The brand showcases an expertise in watch making and their timepieces have adorned the wrist of both men and women since many years.

    An authorized retail partner, Ethos Watch Boutiques shares the brand’s values of authenticity, quality and performance, thus forming an ideal association. With 45 watch boutiques all over the world, Ethos offers a diverse and incredible range from Cartier. Guaranteeing original pieces, for years we have successfully been protecting their patrons from industry malpractices including smuggling of watches, thus building immense goodwill. So whether formal or casual, whatever the occasion you want to accessorize for, Cartier provides you with the perfect selection to choose from.
