  • About Jaeger-LeCoultre Watches

    Based in Le Sentier, with hundreds of inventions and over a thousand calibres to its name, including the world’s smallest calibre, Jaeger-LeCoultre’s relationship with innovation has resulted in the birth of some of the most prized, luxury watches in history. Founded by Antoine LeCoultre in 1833 and ever since, the company has produced over 1,242 different calibres, registered approximately 400 patents and is behind the creation of over hundreds of inventions, why is why they are still a leading player in the watch making world. The brand has always represented itself as a part of a rich and eventful legacy, committed to utmost excellence to reach new heights of innovation. This very philosophy has defined their success for centuries.

    Not only have they mastered the art of creating new technological marvels but have retained their distinct style in almost all of their watches. Jaeger-LeCoultre have designed some of the world’s most complicated wristwatches of the old and modern era, carefully crafted with intricate details to make it perfect that never go unnoticed. If you’re looking to get a Jaeger-LeCoultre watch that truly symbolizes the timeless heritage of the brand, have a look at our collection at Ethos Watch Boutiques. With over 45 stores across India, Ethos is the largest chain of authorized luxury watch boutiques in the nation, offering the widest and most exquisite range of luxury watches. Each watch that we offer comes with the brand warranty and an authorized Ethos stamp and you could be rest assured that the product is 100% original.
