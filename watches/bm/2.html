  • About Baume & Mercier Watches

    With its inception in 1830, Baume & Mercier has carved a niche for itself and is the seventh oldest brand to manufacture Swiss watches. The watches are so designed that they capture authentic moments. Baume & Mercier crafts various timepieces ranging from the more complicated chronographs to the simpler, more elegant dress watches. The brand believes in its signature - “Accept only perfection, only manufactures watches of high quality” for designing and manufacturing watches. After understanding the market for women’s watches they started designing feminine watches from 1940’s. Each timepiece crafted is elegant and perfect and leaves the viewer awestruck, every time one looks at the fascinating designs. Beauty and technical perfection have been their key parameters and they speak of unparalleled precision, grand mechanics and fine quality. The watches primarily showcase traditional, attractive & pleasing to the eye designs combined with sophisticated and reliable mechanics, innovative technologies and practical functionality. The brand encompasses an impressive range of time tellers in the Clifton, Capeland, Classima, Hampton and Linea collection. Baume & Mercier watches have always won awards at the different global Exhibitions or competitions launched by chronometric observatories. In 1919, the firm was rewarded the well known "Poinçon de Genève" or Geneva Hallmark, a token of remarkable craftsmanship and quality.
