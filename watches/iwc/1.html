  • About IWC Watches

    IWC Schaffhausen is considered as a premium brand in the international luxury watch segment and has been creating timepieces of high quality in haute horlogerie. It was founded by watchmaker Ariosto Jones in 1868 and since then they have been manufacturing and distributing perfect mechanical masterpieces. Their watches reflect a fused work of American production techniques and Swiss watchmaking craftsmanship. Innovation and precision are some of the adjectives that can be used to describe the brand watches. Their time keepers resemble a work of art and the brand focus mainly on crafting high quality and classy time tellers. Their watches speak volumes about the brands novel spirit and high engineering skill. IWC watches always stand true to the same high quality standards and are subject to the same attention to detail that they began with in their foremost years. They are synonymous with innovation and refinement, and meet the quality and aesthetic criteria demanded by their audience. They offer a wide range of collection - the pilot’s, Portofino, Aquatimer, Da Vinci, Ingenieur, and Portuguese which is a combination of both classical and traditional methods. IWC watches are considered robust for everyday and professional use. They craft timepieces which are well suited for any occasion and give you a perfect look by adorning your wrist. IWC has established an international reputation with its commitment towards innovation and technical inventiveness.
