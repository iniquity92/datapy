  • About Omega Watches

    A name synonymous to quality and innovation, Omega has been known worldwide for its technology that has redefined the philosophy of watch making. The brand has created some of the most influential timepieces in the history through a proud legacy of commitment to innovation. For them sky is the limit, literally. The brand had participated in the first moon landing in 1962 and then the other 5 lunar landings too. In 1965 Omega Speedmaster chronograph was flight qualified by NASA for all manned space missions. They were the ones to create the first divers watch in 1932 and also the official timekeeper of the Olympic Games. They have been pioneers of creations, the best in their field too. When you are looking for legendary timepieces which speak volumes about a brand then Omega is the place to go to. Omega watches has continuously made significant contributions to watch making technology since its foundation in 1848 by Louis Brandt. The co-axial technology, silicon balance spring, liquid metal, cera gold technology has been excelled by this brand. A premium name in the Swatch group, their watches stand the test of time. What's more, their watches have conquered Hollywood too. The famous James Bond series has been a witness to the protagonist sporting these watches time and again. The first movie in which Pierce Brosnan wore an Omega watch was Golden Eye, released in 1995. He sported an Omega Seamaster Quartz Professional 300m. Then came “Tomorrow Never Dies” in 1997 where James Bond was again shown with an Omega Seamaster 300m. Amazingly the X-rayed Seamaster had a starring role in the opening credits. In 2006 you see the new James Bond, Daniel Craig in a suave stylish avatar yet again sporting the Seamaster Planet Ocean 600mCo-Axial Chronometer. A perfect depiction of the long lasting association of Omega and James Bond came out strongly yet again in Skyfall when Daniel Craig is spotted wearing an Omega Seamaster Aqua Terra. Both the names stand true to their principles of ultra modern design and fashion sensibilities. Omega watches have seen the epitome of success and hence are leaders in the world of horology.
